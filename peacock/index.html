About the App

Peacock empowers users to take control of their time by blocking distracting apps during custom focus windows. Whether you're working, studying, or simply trying to unplug, Peacock helps you stay motivated and on track by letting you choose which apps to restrict and when.

Privacy

Peacock does not collect or share any personal data. All focus session settings and usage data are processed securely and remain on your device.
